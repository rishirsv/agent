Pro-forma adjustments
As discussed in Key Finding 1, certain accounting adjustments are only made at the year end by the Management. We have therefore provided pro-forma adjustments, and presented a pro-forma P&L account for indicative purpose only. Please see more details in Appendix 1.
The school normally provides adjustment for unearned revenue only at the year end resulting in the overstatement of monthly revenue and the understatement of monthly unearned revenue balance. We have therefore proposed the revenue adjustment to exclude the portion of revenue, which should have been recognized in MM 20X3, FY[X]4, FY[X]5 and FY[X]6 accounts. Please see Appendix

for further adjustment details.
In the XM2X period, the school recorded staff costs (e.g., salary and wages) incurred for 8 months up to JMM 20X3. Management confirmed that the school paid staff salary on time.
We have calculated an indicative adjustment of staff expenses for the 11 months period based on a pro-rata basis.
