Comments on unearned revenue
Management informed us that unearned revenue is
recognised
on a yearly basis. All invoices issued during the period will be
recognised
in revenue and subsequently adjusted by the portion that belongs to the
future fiscal years at the end of period.
This indicates that the reported unearned revenue balance per the TB provided as at DD MM 20X3 is understated (and revenue is overstated). We have estimated the unearned revenue based on information provided, and have proposed as additional adjustment to both Quality of earning and Net working capital analysis. Management also informed us that unearned revenue may include the uncollected tuition fee which is part of the outstanding trade and other receivables. The details related to actual cash received in unearned revenue have not been provided by Management. In addition, tuition fee and non-tuition can be refundable indicating that there is a risk that certain portion of unearned revenue may be returned. Please refer to Appendix 2 for the refund policy
.
