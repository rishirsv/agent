Business overview (2/2)
The Company’s selection criteria for a dental practice location includes:
Acquisition Financial Thresholds                                              •    $2.0 million or more in revenue
•    $400,000 or more in EBITDA
Practice-Level Attributes                                                     •    Multiple dentists and hygienists for a diverse pool of revenue producers
•    Strong clinical reputation and practice standards
•    Attractive location and facility types
Partner Dentist Demographics                                                  •    Average age of a Partner Dentist at the time of acquisition of their practice is early 40s
•    Within Dentalcorp, the average age of all dentists is mid-40s
Market Opportunity and Strategic Fit                                          •    Of the approximately 16,000 independently owned dental practices in Canada, management estimates that around 10,000
meet the Company’s Partner Dentist acquisition criteria, with an additional 5,000 suitable for capacity utilization, patient
chart acquisitions, or as supplementary locations for existing Partner Dentists—highlighting significant white space for
continued growth
